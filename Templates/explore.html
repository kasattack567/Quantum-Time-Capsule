What is the Quantum Time Capsule?

The Quantum Time Capsule is a digital project that explores the intersection of time and quantum mechanics. It serves as a repository of knowledge and tools that help users understand and visualize complex scientific concepts related to quantum physics and time.

What Does This Website Do?

This website is designed to guide you through the fundamentals of quantum physics, focusing particularly on how time behaves at the quantum level. You can explore experiments, view visualizations, and learn about the underlying principles of this project in an engaging way.

How It Works

Our website is broken down into key areas:

  • Explore: This section provides an introduction to key concepts and tools that help users grasp the science behind the project.
  • Experiments: Run virtual experiments that simulate quantum phenomena and time dynamics.
  • Archive: Access saved snapshots and historical data related to our work on time and quantum mechanics.
